Malware Infection

You downloaded a program from the internet, and now your computer is acting strange. It's slow, you see weird ads, and websites look different than usual. This might be caused by bad software that can spy on you and steal your information. Be careful about what you download!

What happens:

  • The bad software can steal your passwords and other private things.
  • It can cause your computer to do bad things without you knowing.

Goals:

  1. Figure out what the bad software is.
  2. Protect your important information.

Social Media Got Hacked

You use the same password for many things online. Someone stole your password from one website. Now, they might be able to get into your other accounts too. It's important to use different passwords for everything!

What happens:

  • The bad guys can get into your email, bank accounts, and other important things.
  • They can steal your money or pretend to be you online.

Goals:

  1. Figure out how they stole your password.
  2. Make sure all your online accounts are safe.
